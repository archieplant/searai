2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you create an account and use Recipe Killer AI, we collect:

  • Account Information: Email address, first name, last name, and password (encrypted)
  • Dietary Preferences: Allergies, dislikes, and dietary restrictions you voluntarily provide
  • Referral Source: How you heard about Recipe Killer AI
  • Recipe Photos: Images you upload for recipe analysis
  • Recipe Data: Saved recipes, recent recipes, and complexity preferences

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Number of recipe analyses, saved recipes count, and app interactions
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version
  • Subscription Data: Subscription status, tier (free/premium), and transaction history

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Your precise location or GPS data
  • Your contacts or address book
  • Your phone number (unless you voluntarily provide it)
  • Biometric data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide Core Services: Analyze recipes using AI, generate simplified versions, and save your preferences
  • Personalize Experience: Respect your dietary restrictions and preferences in recipe generation
  • Manage Your Account: Authenticate users, manage subscriptions, and enforce usage limits
  • Community Features: Display your shared recipes in the Discover feed (anonymously, unless you choose otherwise)
  • Improve Our Service: Understand how users interact with the app to make improvements
  • Communication: Send important service updates, subscription notifications, and respond to support requests
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 We DO NOT Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5.2 Community Features (Discover Feed)

If you choose to share recipes to the Discover feed:

  • Your recipe photo, name, and complexity level become publicly visible to all app users
  • Your sharing is anonymous by default (we don't display your name or profile)
  • You can control whether to share each recipe individually

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government request, or to:

  • Protect our legal rights
  • Prevent fraud or security threats
  • Comply with law enforcement requests

6. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active, plus 90 days after deletion
  • Recipe Photos: Retained as long as you keep the recipe saved; deleted when you delete the recipe
  • Discover Feed Posts: Retained for 90 days after you remove them from the feed
  • Subscription History: Retained for 7 years for tax and accounting purposes
  • OpenAI Data: Automatically deleted after 30 days by OpenAI

7. Your Rights (GDPR & UK Data Protection)

If you are in the UK or European Economic Area, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction: Request we limit how we use your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to certain types of processing
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time (doesn't affect prior processing)

To exercise these rights, email us at archieplant02@hotmail.co.uk

8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Password Security: Passwords are hashed and salted using bcrypt
  • Database Security: Row-level security (RLS) policies on Supabase
  • Access Controls: Limited employee/developer access to production data

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
