Content Policy & Community Guidelines

Last Updated: November 18, 2025

1. Our Community Values

Recipe Killer AI is a community for food lovers to discover, share, and simplify recipes. We want everyone to feel welcome, safe, and inspired. These guidelines help maintain a positive environment for all users.

2. What You CAN Share

✅ Acceptable Content:
  • Recipe Photos: Images of dishes you've cooked or want to cook
  • Food Photography: Photos from restaurants, cookbooks, or websites (for personal recipe analysis)
  • Home-Cooked Meals: Your own creations and cooking experiments
  • Recipe Variations: Different complexity levels of the same dish
  • International Cuisine: Recipes from any culture or country
  • Dietary Adaptations: Vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, or other dietary versions

3. What You CANNOT Share

❌ Prohibited Content:
  • Offensive Content: Racist, sexist, homophobic, or discriminatory content
  • Explicit Content: Pornographic, sexually explicit, or obscene material
  • Violence or Gore: Graphic images of violence, injury, or death
  • Dangerous Content: Recipes promoting eating disorders, unsafe food practices, or harmful substances
  • Illegal Content: Content promoting illegal activities, drugs, or weapons
  • Spam: Repetitive, irrelevant, or promotional content
  • Copyrighted Material: Content you don't have rights to share (see section below)
  • Misleading Content: Fake recipes, misinformation, or deliberately incorrect information
  • Personal Information: Sharing others' private information without consent
  • Harassment: Content targeting, bullying, or harassing other users

4. Copyright and Intellectual Property

4.1 Respect Copyright

When sharing recipes to the Discover feed:

  • Only share photos you took yourself or have permission to share
  • Don't upload copyrighted images from professional photographers without permission
  • If adapting a recipe from a cookbook or website, you may analyze it for personal use, but be mindful when sharing publicly
  • Recipe ideas and concepts generally aren't copyrightable, but exact text and photos often are

4.2 Fair Use for Personal Analysis

You may upload copyrighted recipe photos or text for personal AI analysis (this is generally considered fair use for personal, transformative purposes). However, sharing someone else's copyrighted photo publicly in the Discover feed may not be protected.

4.3 DMCA Takedown Requests

If you believe content in Recipe Killer AI infringes your copyright, contact us at archieplant02@hotmail.co.uk with:

  • Description of copyrighted work
  • URL or screenshot of infringing content
  • Your contact information
  • Statement of good faith belief
  • Statement of accuracy and authority

We will investigate and remove infringing content within 48 hours.

5. Community Behavior

5.1 Be Respectful

  • Treat all users with kindness and respect
  • Celebrate diverse cuisines and cooking styles
  • Avoid judgmental or condescending comments
  • Respect different dietary choices and restrictions

5.2 Be Authentic

  • Share genuine recipes and experiences
  • Don't manipulate or game the system
  • Don't create multiple accounts to spam content

5.3 Be Constructive

  • Share helpful recipes and variations
  • Focus on food, not negativity
  • If you wouldn't say it in a real kitchen, don't say it here

6. Reporting Content

If you see content that violates these guidelines:

  • Use the "Report" button (coming soon in-app)
  • Email us at archieplant02@hotmail.co.uk with screenshot and description
  • We review reports within 48 hours

False or malicious reports may result in account restrictions.

7. Enforcement

7.1 Content Removal

We reserve the right to remove content that violates these guidelines without notice. Removed content includes:

  • Individual recipe posts
  • Photos or images
  • Any user-generated content

7.2 Account Actions

Violations may result in:

  • First Offense: Warning and content removal
  • Second Offense: Temporary suspension (7-30 days)
  • Third Offense or Severe Violation: Permanent account ban

Severe violations (illegal content, harassment, spam) may result in immediate permanent ban.

7.3 Appeals

If you believe your content was removed or account banned in error:

  • Email archieplant02@hotmail.co.uk within 30 days
  • Include your username and explanation
  • We will review and respond within 7 business days

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update these Community Guidelines as the app evolves. Changes will be posted here with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use after changes indicates acceptance.
